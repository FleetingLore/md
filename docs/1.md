## 1.4  放大电路模型

**1. 模拟信号放大**

$$

$$

$$

\tag{1.4.3}
$$

$$

**2. 放大电路模型**

$$
v _o = A _{v o} v _i \frac{R _L}{R _L + R _o}
\tag{1.4.5}
$$

$$
A _v = \frac{v _o}{v _i} = A _{v o} \frac{R _L}{R _L + R _o}
\tag{1.4.6}
$$

$$
v _i = v _s \frac{R _i}{R _{s i} + R _i}
\tag{1.4.7}
$$

$$
i _o = A _{i s} i _i \frac{R _o}{R _L + R _o}
\tag{1.4.8}
$$

$$
A _i = \frac{i _o}{i _i} = A _{i s} \frac{R _o}{R _L + R _o}
\tag{1.4.9}
$$

$$
i _i = i _s \frac{R _{s i}}{R _{s i} + R _i}
\tag{1.4.10}
$$

$$
A _{v o} v _i = A _{i s} i _i R _o = A _{i s} \frac{v _i}{R _i} R _o
\tag{1.4.11}
$$
